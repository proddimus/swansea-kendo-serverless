Kendo in Swansea

Kendo meaning "Way of the Sword", is the modern Japanese martial art of sword-fencing based on traditional Japanese swordsmanship, or Kenjutsu. Kendo is a physically and mentally challenging activity that combines strong martial arts values with sport-like physical elements.

Training times and locations can be viewed on the Training Times page.

Beginner Classes - learn how to do Kendo

We run beginner classes throughout the year in January, April and September. If you're interested in learning more about kendo and would like to come along to a beginner class please send us a mail or contact us at our Facebook page .
